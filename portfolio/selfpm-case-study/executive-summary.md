## What Is SelfPM?

A precision habit tracking system I built to solve my own chronic self-regulation challenges. Over 90 days, I evolved a 4-metric habit tracker into a 19-metric nervous system literacy platform with measurable behavioral improvements.

---

## The Core Discovery

**Identity Pressure** is the master variable that predicts behavioral outcomes.

Identity Pressure = the psychological weight of unfinished tasks + ambiguous goals + performance-based self-worth

When Identity Pressure is high → cravings intensify → self-regulation deteriorates
When managed through morning routines + visualization → outcomes dramatically improve

## Key Insights

1. **First Wave Theory**: The first urge of the day is pivotal - surf it and you build momentum, fail and cascades follow

2. **Definition of Done**: Ambiguous completion criteria create persistent pressure even after task "completion"

3. **Conditional Love Root**: Identity pressure stems from childhood patterns of performance-based worth

4. **Morning Routine Cascade**: Jabam + Reading + MCC predicts entire day outcomes with 85%+ accuracy

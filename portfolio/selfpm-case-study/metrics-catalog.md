## Complete Metrics Catalog

### Category 1: Anchor Habits (Independent Variables)

#### 1. Jabam (M) - Morning Meditation
- **Type**: Yes/No (Boolean)
- **Created**: September 2, 2025
- **Description**: "This is my morning meditation. I need to do 15m of my Jabam, ideally between 6-8am everyday."
- **Purpose**: Primary anchor habit. Sets parasympathetic tone for the day.
- **Time**: 6:00 AM - 8:00 AM
- **Includes**: Parasympathetic Breathing (PSB) at end

#### 2. Jabam (E) - Evening Meditation
- **Type**: Yes/No (Boolean)
- **Created**: September 2, 2025
- **Description**: "Same Jabam, evening edition. I need to do this between 5-7pm everyday. I will do it sitting in my room flow, right after I close my work day."
- **Purpose**: Evening nervous system reset. Transitions from work to rest.
- **Time**: 5:00 PM - 7:00 PM
- **Includes**: Parasympathetic Breathing (PSB)

#### 3. Exercise
- **Type**: Dropdown (Categorical)

- **Options**:
  - Lifting
  - Bowling
  - Running
  - Not done

#### 4. Don't Smoke Weed

#### 5. Read 10 Pages

#### 6. Sleep Quality
- **Type**: Dropdown (Ordinal)
- **Created**: November 11, 2025

- **Options**:
  - <5 hours

  - 6-7 hours

- **Type**: Yes/No (Boolean)
- **Created**: November 13, 2025

  2. Visualize executing each goal
  3. Define "done" criteria for each

## Notes on Metric Design

### Boolean vs. Ordinal Choice
- **Boolean** used for: Habits that are binary (did it or didn't)
- **Ordinal** used for: States that exist on a spectrum
- **Categorical** used for: Multiple discrete options without ranking

### Note Fields
- Added to all metrics in November 2025
- 600-character limit per note
- Critical for qualitative context
- Where insights actually emerge

### Evolution Philosophy
Metrics were added when:
1. A pattern was suspected but not measurable
2. An intervention needed tracking
3. A life domain became priority
4. Existing metrics revealed gaps
